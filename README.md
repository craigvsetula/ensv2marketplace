## The ENS Taxonomy

The **[30-Category, 275-Subcategory Taxonomy](https://www.ensv2marketplace.com/pricing-guide.html)** is the classification backbone of the portfolio.

Each subcategory defines a specific function within a vertical. Domain assignment is deterministic ‚Äî driven by what the name does, not what it sounds like.

### The Identity Chain ‚Äî Semantic Relationships

One of the most important structural patterns in the taxonomy is the identity-to-policy chain:

```
Identity ‚Üí Auth ‚Üí AuthZ ‚Üí Access ‚Üí Permission ‚Üí Policy
```

- **Identity** ‚Äî Who you are (`federal-id.eth`, `national-id.eth`, `stateid.eth`)
- **Auth** ‚Äî Prove it (`authsystem.eth`, `authgateway.eth`)
- **AuthZ** ‚Äî What you can do (`authzengine.eth`, `permissionregistry.eth`)
- **Access** ‚Äî Open the door (`accessorchestrator.eth`, `accessgateway.eth`)
- **Permission** ‚Äî Rules applied (`permissionledger.eth`, `policyengine.eth`)
- **Policy** ‚Äî Governance layer (`compliancepolicy.eth`, `auditengine.eth`)
