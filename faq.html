General
What is an ENS domain?
ENS (Ethereum Name Service) domains are human-readable names for Ethereum addresses, like yourname.eth. They work like DNS for Web3 - instead of sharing a long hex address, you can use a simple name for payments, websites, and identity.
How many domains does BuyETHaddress have?
We own 1,600+ premium ENS domains focused on real-world utility. Infrastructure, DeFi, validators, AI agents, compliance, tax, and protocol-level names. Every domain acquired for $5-6 registration during bear market conditions.
Are these domains actually owned by you?
Yes, 100%. Every domain is secured in a hardware wallet (Ledger). You can verify ownership on-chain. We don't list domains we don't own.
What makes your domains "premium"?
We focus on infrastructure and utility terms - the names protocols actually need: liquidationbot, validatorregistry, zkengine, accountabstraction, slashing, etc. These aren't speculation names - they're functional infrastructure for serious Web3 builders.

Subdomain Leasing
Can I lease instead of buying?
Absolutely. Subdomain leasing is our specialty. Get premium branding like yourprotocol.zkengine.eth for a fraction of ownership costs. Check our subdomain leasing page for details.
How does subdomain leasing work?
You get full control of a subdomain under our premium parent domain. Set your own records, use it for wallets, websites, contracts - functionally equivalent to owning a primary ENS name. We retain the parent domain ownership.
What are the lease terms?
Annual leases. Pay per year, renew as long as you want. Pricing locked at lease initiation - no surprise increases. Cancel anytime, no long-term commitments required. Lease prices range from $50-$1,500/year based on parent domain value.
What happens if I stop paying?
Your subdomain expires and control returns to us. No penalties, no drama. Just like ENS registration renewal - pay to keep it, stop paying and it lapses.
Technical & Transfers
How is the domain transfer done?
Through the ENS Manager interface. We transfer the domain to your address, you accept it. No wallet connection to our site - all transfers happen on-chain through official ENS tooling. Safe, verifiable, standard process.
Do I need a special wallet?
Any Ethereum wallet works. MetaMask, Rainbow, Ledger, Trezor - if it holds ETH, it can hold ENS domains. Just needs to support the ENS protocol (basically all do).
What are ENS renewal fees?
ENS charges annual renewal fees: $5/year for 5+ character domains, $160/year for 4-character, $640/year for 3-character. These go to ENS DAO, not us. After you buy a domain from us, you're responsible for renewals.
Can I use the domain immediately after purchase?
Yes. Once transferred, it's yours. Set it as your primary name, point it to contracts, use it for payments - full control immediately.
